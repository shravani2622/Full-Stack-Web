Project1:Prediction Of Water Quality Using Machine Learning

Freshwater is a critical resource for agriculture and industry's survival. Examination of water quality is a fundamental stage in the administration of freshwater assets. As indicated by the World Health Organization's yearly report, many individuals are getting sick or some are dead due to the lack of safe drinking water, especially pregnant ladies and kids. It is critical to test the quality of water prior to involving it for any reason, whether it is for animal watering, chemical spraying(Pesticides etc), or drinking water. Water quality testing is a strategy for finding clean drinking water. Accordingly, appropriate water monitoring is basic for safe, clean, and sterile water. Water testing is fundamental for looking at the legitimate working of water sources, testing the safety of drinking water, identifying disease outbreaks, and approving methodology and safeguard activites.Water quality is a proportion of a water's readiness for a specific utilize in view of physical, chemical,and biological qualities.

Predicting groundwater availability is important to water sustainability and drought mitigation.Groundwater is one of the basic source, which is used for several purposes throughout the world such as for irrigation, drinking and industrial use. The presence of potable ground water suggests that the water is fit for human consumption or not. WQI is an important technique for demarcating the water quality and its suitable for drinking purpose. The utilization of machine learning algorithms like Gradient Boosting Classifier algorithm, Random Forest algorithm, Decision Tree algorithm in water research for accurate results.
